A $3 \times 3$ matrix has eigenvalues $1, 2, 3$. What is $\text{tr}(A)$ and $\det(A)$?

<details>
<summary>Answer</summary>

$\text{tr}(A) = 1 + 2 + 3 = 6$ (sum of eigenvalues equals trace).
$\det(A) = 1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 = 6$ (product of eigenvalues equals determinant).
</details>
