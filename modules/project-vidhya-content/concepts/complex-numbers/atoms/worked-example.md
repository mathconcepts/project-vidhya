**Problem:** Compute $(2+3i)(1-2i)$ and write in $a+bi$ form.

---

**Step 1 — Distribute (FOIL).** $(2+3i)(1-2i) = 2(1) + 2(-2i) + 3i(1) + 3i(-2i)$.

---

**Step 2 — Simplify each term.** $= 2 - 4i + 3i - 6i^2$.

---

**Step 3 — Use $i^2 = -1$.** $-6i^2 = -6(-1) = 6$. So $= 2 - 4i + 3i + 6$.

---

**Step 4 — Collect real and imaginary parts.** Real: $2 + 6 = 8$. Imaginary: $-4i + 3i = -i$. Answer: $\boxed{8 - i}$.
