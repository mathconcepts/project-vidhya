## 📌 Revision Strategy

### Formula Sheet Method
- Print all 10 formula sheets by Week 4
- Review during breakfast/commute (10 min/day)
- Re-derive from memory each Saturday

### Error Log
- Maintain a notebook of wrong answers
- Note: What concept did I miss?
- Revisit that concept the same evening

### Spaced Repetition
- Week 1 topics → Revisit in Week 6 briefly
- Week 2 topics → Revisit in Week 7 briefly
- (Continue the pattern)

### 48-Hour Rule
Any concept you don't understand in 48 hours → **Ask Sage immediately**. Don't move on with gaps.
